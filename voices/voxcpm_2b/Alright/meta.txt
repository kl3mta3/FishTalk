This is not a traditional memoir. Yes, I tell stories from the past, but I have no interest in nostalgia, sentimentality, or the retirement that most memoirs require. This is not an advice book either. Although I like preachers, I'm not here to preach and tell you what to do. This is an approach book. I am here to share stories insights and philosophies that can be objectively understood, and if you choose subjectively adopted by the changing your reality, or changing how to use.
